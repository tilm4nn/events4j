public interface Event3<T1,T2,T3>

An Event3 is a source of signals and/or information that can be observed by subscribing Action3 instances to it. When an Event3 signals all subscribed Action3s are invoked. The order of of the invocation of the Action3s and the behavior in case of an RuntimeException during the invocation of an Action3 depends on the concrete Event3 implementation.
