public interface Event0

An Event0 is a source of signals that can be observed by subscribing Action0 instances to it. When an Event0 signals all subscribed Action0s are invoked. The order of of the invocation of the Action0s and the behavior in case of an RuntimeException during the invocation of an Action0 depends on the concrete Event0 implementation.
