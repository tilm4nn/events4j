public interface Event2<T1,T2>

An Event2 is a source of signals and/or information that can be observed by subscribing Action2 instances to it. When an Event2 signals all subscribed Action2s are invoked. The order of of the invocation of the Action2s and the behavior in case of an RuntimeException during the invocation of an Action2 depends on the concrete Event2 implementation.
